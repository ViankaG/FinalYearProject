A Guide To Dementia

This is the home page for this website. This website is made up of two pages. The blue button on the left titled "Information About Types of Dementia" will take you to the first page, which includes information on different types of dementia. The blue button on the right titled "Stories and Advice From People Living With Dementia" will take you to the second page, which includes stories and advice from people living with dementia.
