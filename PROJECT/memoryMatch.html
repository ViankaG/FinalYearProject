Memory Match Game

Aim of the game:
To match all the cards in the deck with their pairs.
Instructions
1. Click on two cards from the deck below
2. If the images on the cards are the same, this means they are matched and will not flip back over. If the images on the cards are not the same, then both cards will shake to indicate that they have not been matched and they will flip back over.
3. Keep clicking on two cards at a time until all cards are matched with their pairs
4. The game will end when all pairs of cards have been matched. All cards will flip over, the cards will be shuffled and the game will begin again.
